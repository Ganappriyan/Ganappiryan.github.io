Scott Lang (Ant-Man)

Marvel Cinematic Universe

Scott Lang is a fictional character portrayed by Paul Rudd in the MCU and known commonly by his alias, Ant-Man.

Origins

Scott Lang graduated from MIT, but turned to a life of crime to punish a corporation that had swindled its customers and got arrested. While in prison, his wife Maggie leaves him with their daughter, Cassie.

Becoming Ant-Man

After released from prison in 2015, unable to hold a legal job because of his criminal record, Lang agrees to join his best friend Luis and his crew to commit last burglary. It only gave them only a suit which Lang was manipulated to steal, to test his skills by Hank Pim.

Later Hank and Lang joined together to steal Yellow Jacket Suit, from his former protégé, Darren Cross, who reverse engineered Hank's Pym Particles Technology.
